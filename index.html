About me

“ First, solve the problem. Then, write the code. ”

I have wanted to be many things in life since I was a child. Drawn initially to the sciences, I feel in love with learning about the natural world we live in and beyond. Once I reached my first semester as an undergraduate my fascniation with logic and debate steered me into my first college mathematics course - multivariable calculus. Once I reached my sophmore year it took no time at all before I became transfixed by the elegance and intrinsic beauty found throughout my first post-calculus math courses. As the complexity continued to grow while digging deeper into the realm of abstract mathematics, I found my thought processes, world views, and my approach to dealing with issues in life all began to change. For the sake of brevity, mathematics became one of my greatest passions in life. Even after my graduation I continued to read books on subfields of math (there are quite a lot of them) in my free time that I never had the opportunity to fit into my official course schedule. This notwithstanding, I eventually decided that I wanted to work in industry where I could apply the knowledge and skillsets that I both love so much and dedicated so much time and effort into aquiering. Although it came of no surprise, particularly given the tech world we now inhabit, I realized that the best approach to achieve my goal would involve further study of computer programing. And I've been on the journey ever since. Even today I believe that no matter where I end up or what I do professionally in life I cannot think of a more relivant, practical field to invest myself in. It is what I have been focusing on ever since, and is what I am focusing on today.

I am currently a student in a 9 week accelerated Java track at ElevenFifty academy in Fishers IN. I am lifelong learner by nature and fully expect to continue growing as developer. Currently for building softwear I utilize Python, R, MatLab, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript and Java programing languages.
